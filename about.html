✦   About Dana   ✦

Hi, I'm Dana.

A creator obsessed with the big in the little — and the woman behind the brand.

DK

The girl who found her thing.

For 7+ years, I've been chasing one question: What if the thing you already have is actually enough?

I started where most creators start — with too much heart and not enough resources. No fancy gear. No big budgets. Just a deep belief that there's more in raw moments than people give them credit for.

That belief became my craft. Today I work as a creative director, video editor, and scriptwriter for creators, brands, and ministries — helping them see and tell the story that was already there.

And because I learned the hard way, I teach too. Workshops, keynotes, mentorships — wherever someone is trying to figure out their next step in this creative life, I want to be a voice that says: you already have what you need.

"I create something big out of something little. Give me raw footage, scattered ideas, limited resources — I'll turn them into stories that speak."

— Dana
