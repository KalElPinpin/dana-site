✦   About Dana   ✦

I'm so glad you're here.

Since I was 15, I've been obsessed with one thing — finding the gold buried inside small, raw moments. The kind of stories that get overlooked, dismissed, or thrown away because they don't seem "ready" yet.

As a creative director, video editor, and scriptwriter for creators, brands, and ministries, I turn ordinary footage and scattered ideas into work that actually moves people. And when I'm not creating, I'm teaching others how to do the same.

Welcome to my corner of the internet — a space for creatives, dreamers, and stewards of small beginnings.

— Dana
