I don't just create. I teach.

From keynotes to intimate workshops, I help aspiring creators discover the value in what they already have — and build a real career from it.

Dana on stage

Speaking is my love language.

There's nothing like a roomful of dreamers who are one mindset shift away from breakthrough. I get to be that voice — the one that says you already have what you need.

I speak at conferences, retreats, university events, ministry gatherings, and creator meetups across the Philippines (and globally, via livestream). Every talk is custom-built for your audience — no copy-paste keynotes, no generic content.

If you're planning an event and want a speaker who'll meet your audience where they are and walk them somewhere meaningful, I'd love to talk.
